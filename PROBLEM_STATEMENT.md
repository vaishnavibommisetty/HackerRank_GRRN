# GRRN - Global Resource Redistribution Network
## Hackathon Problem Statement

### 🌍 **Problem Overview**

In today's world, millions of people face resource scarcity while tons of usable resources go to waste daily. Restaurants discard surplus food, pharmacies expire unused medicines, and individuals have unwanted items that could benefit others. Simultaneously, NGOs and charitable organizations struggle to meet the needs of communities they serve due to resource shortages and inefficient distribution systems.

### 📊 **Current Challenges**

1. **Resource Waste**
   - Restaurants waste 40% of prepared food daily
   - Pharmacies discard expired but usable medications
   - Individuals have unused items (clothing, electronics, books, toys)
   - No centralized system to connect surplus with need

2. **Distribution Inefficiency**
   - NGOs struggle to source resources consistently
   - Manual coordination processes are time-consuming
   - No real-time visibility of available resources
   - Geographic mismatch between resources and needs

3. **Accessibility Barriers**
   - Complex donation processes discourage potential donors
   - Limited awareness of NGO needs
   - No tracking of resource impact
   - Trust and transparency issues

### 🎯 **Project Mission**

**Build a digital platform that seamlessly connects resource surplus with resource need, creating an efficient ecosystem for resource redistribution.**

### 👥 **Target Users**

**Primary Users:**
- **Resource Donors**: Restaurants, pharmacies, individuals with surplus
- **Resource Recipients**: NGOs, community organizations, charitable institutions
- **Beneficiaries**: Communities in need, underserved populations

**Secondary Users:**
- **Volunteers**: Resource transport and distribution
- **Administrators**: Platform management and oversight

### 🛠 **Core Features Required**

#### **1. Smart Resource Matching**
- **Source-Based Filtering**: Restaurant → Food, Pharmacy → Medicine, Personal → All
- **Intelligent Allocation**: Route resources to appropriate NGOs based on type and location
- **Real-Time Inventory**: Live tracking of available resources
- **Demand Forecasting**: Predict resource needs based on historical data

#### **2. User-Friendly Donation System**
- **Multi-Source Support**: Restaurant, Pharmacy, Personal donations
- **Resource Categories**: Food, Medicine, Clothing, Books, Toys, Electronics, Other
- **Visual Interface**: Icon-based resource selection
- **Mobile Responsive**: Accessible on all devices
- **Quick Donation**: 3-step process (Source → Resource → Location)

#### **3. NGO Management Dashboard**
- **Resource Visibility**: Real-time view of available resources
- **Allocation Tracking**: Monitor resource distribution
- **Impact Metrics**: Track lives helped, communities served
- **Communication Tools**: Coordinate with donors and volunteers

#### **4. Trust & Transparency**
- **Resource Verification**: Quality and quantity validation
- **Donor Recognition**: Acknowledge contributions publicly
- **Impact Stories**: Share success stories and testimonials
- **Audit Trail**: Complete transaction history
